
--------------------------------------- Const

             Σ(n ℓ̂) ≡ just T
------------------------------------------------- Addr
    Γ ; Σ ; gc ⊢ addr n ℓ̂ ⇐ Ref (T of ℓ̂) of ℓ

         Γ ∋ x ⦂ A
           A <: B
---------------------------------- Var
    Γ ; Σ ; gc ⊢ ` x ⇐ B

           A ∷ Γ ; Σ ; g ⊢ N ⇐ B
---------------------------------------------- Lambda
    Γ ; Σ ; gc ⊢ ƛ N ⇐ ⟦ g ⟧ A → B of ℓ

  Γ ; Σ ; pc ⊢ L ⇐ ⟦ ℓᶜ ⟧ A → B of ℓ
  Γ ; Σ ; pc ⊢ M ⇐ A
       pc′ ⋎ ℓ ≼ ℓᶜ
      C = stamp B ℓ
--------------------------------------- App
  Γ ; Σ ; pc ⊢ app L M ℓᶜ A B ℓ ⇐ C

    Γ ; Σ ; gc ⊢ L ⇐ ⟦ ⋆ ⟧ A ⇒ B of g
    Γ ; Σ ; gc ⊢ M ⇐ A
      C = stamp B g
--------------------------------------- App!
    Γ ; Σ ; gc ⊢ app! L M A B g ⇐ C
